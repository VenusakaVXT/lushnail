Best Practices for Ghost Buttons:

  • • Use on colored, gradient, or image backgrounds for better visibility
  • • Perfect for secondary actions that shouldn't compete with primary buttons
  • • Add backdrop-blur-sm for better readability on busy backgrounds
  • • Ensure sufficient contrast between text/border and background
  • • Great for navigation, modals, and hero sections
