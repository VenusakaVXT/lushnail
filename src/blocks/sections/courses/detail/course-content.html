COURSE DESCRIPTION

BASIC NAILS COURSE
SOLID FOUNDATION FOR BEGINNERS

Want to enter the nail industry but don't know where to start? The Basic Nails Course is designed specifically for beginners, helping you build a solid technical foundation from the very beginning, creating a strong stepping stone to develop professionally and start earning income from the profession.

What will you learn?

Introduction to nail profession, safety and hygiene when providing services
Understanding nail structure, condition and proper nail care methods
Basic techniques: cuticle care, filing, regular polish, basic gel polish
Creating beautiful nail shapes: square, round, simple oval
Error correction techniques: polish smudges, gel lifting, nail gaps
Complete client service process A-Z: consultation - execution - post-service care

Who is this course suitable for?

100% newbie with no experience, want to learn nail profession from the beginning

People who want to learn quickly to have a stable job and start earning income

Young people aim to develop a long-term nail career from basic to professional
