OUR STORY

Lush Nail Academy is a specialized nail training academy, built with the vision to become a leading center in the field of teaching Nail Design, Nail Box, and Permanent Nail according to international standards. We provide a systematic, modern learning environment and practical orientation, helping students develop professional skills and aesthetic thinking according to US-UK standards.

The team of instructors at Lush Nail are all experts with many years of experience in the industry, having worked and learned directly from the US - UK - Canada markets. With the "hands-on" teaching method, instructors closely accompany students, ensuring that each student masters the precise techniques, operations, and professional working style required by international salons.

Each year, Lush Nail Academy proudly trains over 500 graduates who confidently enter the domestic and international labor market. Many students have successfully worked in the US, UK, Canada, or become independent salon owners with stable incomes. The success of our students is the clearest proof of Lush Nail's training quality and reputation in the market.

More than just a vocational training center, Lush Nail Academy is also a place to build your career foundation. We offer flexible online - offline learning paths, career guidance support, international standard professional conduct training, and continuous practical opportunities.
